Le Plato

App design - School project

Le Plato is a public institution responsible for organizing the representation of scenographic works.

As part of my degree I created the design of the application ‘Le Plato’ whose purpose was to show the dynamism and contemporaneity and highlight the exchange and sharing that this place offers, but also transversal and eclectic programming that allows everyone to satisfy his curiosity, and finally to show the avant-garde settled behind this institution.
