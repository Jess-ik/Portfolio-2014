Neno - TV Channel

TV Branding - School project

NENO is an edutainment channel for 2-6 year olds that offers learning through leisure (crafts, cooking …).

As part of my degree I had to make the visual identity, jingles, idents, a generic for a TV show…

The aim was to show parents that this chain will allow children to grow up, to awake up and to learn by being active while having fun, and attract children to this new channel.
