24H de l'INSA - Festival

Print - School project

As part of the 41st edition of the festival "24h de l'INSA", a competition was organized to create posters and other media. This project was done in collaboration with Charlotte MASSANES

The aim was to target families in the day visuals, and students in the nights ones, without forget the spirit of the festival and its origin : a 24H bike race around the campus.
